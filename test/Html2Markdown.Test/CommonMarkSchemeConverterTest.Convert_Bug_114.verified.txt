## Support

This project will currently convert the following HTML tags:-

*   `<a>`
*   `<strong>`
*   `<b>`
*   `<em>`
*   `<i>`
*   `<br>`
*   `<code>`
*   `<h1>`
*   `<h2>`
*   `<h3>`
*   `<h4>`
*   `<h5>`
*   `<h6>`
*   `<blockquote>`
*   `<img>`
*   `<hr>`
*   `<p>`
*   `<pre>`
*   `<ul>`
*   `<ol>`
